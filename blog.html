Client Deadlines in the Rain

Rainstorms hit harder in Sitio Kawayanan. When the roof starts rattling, I know the WiFi is preparing to abandon me. Deadlines don't care about the weather though. So I sit there, typing at full speed, trying to push commits before the signal collapses.

Still, there's charm in fighting through it. Every finished project feels like a small victory against nature's mood swings. And honestly? I think these messy setups make the stories better.
